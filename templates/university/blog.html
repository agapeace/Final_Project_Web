Student Government Committees

Monday

The Academics & Co-ops

The Academics & Co-ops Committee discusses and proposes solutions on issues relating to academic and co-op affairs as well as promote ideas for improvement at RIT.

Tuesday

Facilities, Parking and Pransportation

Facilities looks at the buildings, operating times, and furnishings on campus. All issues related to parking and transportation services at RIT fall under this committee.

Thursday

Student affairs

Taking on many of the costs associated with improving student life, some of the ongoing expenses of the student affairs committee include services such as improving counseling and psychological services and setting up a study abroad scholarship fund.

Friday

Sustainability

One of Student Government’s largest committees, the Sustainability committee helps look at ways to increase sustainable actions on campus

Services

2014

Miyapedia

MiyaPedia is the place to go for information about the Miyagi community. Created for students by students, MiyaPedia was originally project to create an online resource for new students.

2015

Finance Requests

Student Government provides funding to clubs and organizations through the Application for Additional Funds (AFAF) process. Funds are available through SG Finance Committee, Greek Finance Committee, and Sports Federation.

2015

Legal Aid

Student Government has an attorney who is available to provide free legal advice to students. Sign up for an appointment by visiting or calling the SG office or through the CampusGroups app.

MIYAGI

Miyagi is one of the leading universities in Japan

INTELLECTUAL ELITE OF JAPAN

Living in a comfortable environment (amenities), and having heartwarming human relationships (hospitality), are wishes of all human beings. In order to realize communities with comforts and a heartwarming environment, Miyagi University explores the conditions and meanings of “hospitality” and “amenity”. Accordingly, MYU promotes education and research based on “advanced practical science”, integrating and unifying various disciplines and technologies in order to respond to demands of society.
